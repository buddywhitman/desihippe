Jobs, power and purpose in an age of accelerating intelligence. Not doom, not hype - just a careful look at incentives, dignity and what work used to give us.

### The Incentive Loop

Capitalism thrives on productivity. But when intelligence becomes abundant and virtually free, the classical relationship between labor, capital, and consumption breaks down.

1. **Labor Substitution**: Machines no longer just automate hands; they automate minds.
2. **Capital Concentration**: The value of intelligence accrues to those who own the models.
3. **The Consumption Crisis**: If humans do not earn wages, who buys the products of automated abundance?

We must rethink dignity and work before the loop closes on us.
