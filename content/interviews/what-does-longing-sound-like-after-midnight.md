A talk about ghazals, house music, memory, exile and the strange democracy of a shared chorus.

### The Mid-night Chorus

In a quiet studio in Delhi, we explore the sonic history of longing.

- **Ghazals as Maps**: How classical poetry maps the geography of absence.
- **House Music**: The repetitive beat as a communal trance, a modern ritual for the lonely.
- **Exile and Home**: Sound as the only portable homeland for displaced populations.
