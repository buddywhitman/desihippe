On code, incentives, attention, open knowledge and the quiet violence of interfaces that make people smaller.

### The Architecture of Capture

An interview with a veteran software engineer from Bengaluru on the ethics of modern product design.

* **Attention Farming**: How metrics like Daily Active Users (DAU) incentivize addictive design patterns.
* **The Invisible Friction**: How interfaces can either respect human agency or treat humans as resources to be mined.
* **Alternative Paths**: Open source, decentralized protocols, and local-first software.
