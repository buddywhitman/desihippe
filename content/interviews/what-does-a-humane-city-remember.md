A conversation about shaded streets, water, material honesty and why concrete can feel lonely when it forgets the body.

### Shaded Streets and Human Scales

We sat down with an architect from Auroville to discuss how design shapes our inner lives.

> "A street is not a sewer for cars. It is a room with the sky for a ceiling. If you shade it, people will walk. If you walk, you will talk. If you talk, you are no longer strangers."

We discuss water conservation, local stones, and the spiritual cost of cheap materials.
