A note on mornings, phones, inherited anxieties and the radical act of not beginning the day as a consumer.

### The First Hour

Do not let the world rush in before you are fully awake. The notifications on your lockscreen are other people's priorities, not your life.

Take fifteen minutes. Look at the light on the wall. Drink your water. Remember who you are before you remember what you have to do.
