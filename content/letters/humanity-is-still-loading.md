A small meditation on errors, updates, compassion and the unfinished software of moral life.

### The Unfinished Creature

We are quick to judge ourselves and others as complete, static entities. But moral development is a slow, iterative process.

Be patient with the bugs. The code is old, the environment is changing, and the patch is still being written.
