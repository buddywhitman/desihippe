Why charismatic certainty is seductive, why humility is harder and how groups can stay alive without becoming cages.

### The Allure of Certainty

It is easy to follow someone who promises they know the way. It relieves us of the heavy work of thinking. But a room of disciples is always silent, except for one voice.

We prefer the messy, slow, democratic friction of shared doubts.
